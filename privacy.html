WestminsterSim — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 June 2026

WestminsterSim ("the app") is a single‑player political simulation game developed by Meridian Analytica LTD. This is the whole policy, and the short version is the whole story: the app collects no data.

No data collected

The app does not collect, store on a server, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are:

  • No user accounts and no sign‑in.
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind.
  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No third‑party services that receive your data.
  • No network requests — the app runs entirely on your device.

Your game save stays on your device

Your career progress is saved locally on your device using standard on‑device app storage. This save never leaves your device, is never sent to us or anyone else, and cannot be accessed by the developer. Deleting the app removes the save.

Device permissions

The app does not request access to sensitive device features such as your location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or files.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no information from anyone, it collects nothing from children. The game contains no real‑money purchases, advertising, or communication features.

Content

WestminsterSim is a work of fiction. All characters and storylines are fictional. Real UK political party names are referenced for setting only; no real individuals are depicted.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
